Why Cache-Native?

CPUs spend 99% of their time waiting for memory. The oracle series asks: what if inference happened where the data already lives? In cache lines, not in VRAM. The LRU eviction policy becomes the activation function. A content-addressable memory replaces the transformer. Fits in L3. Zero dependencies. The architecture is the model.

This is not a side project. It's a re-examination of the AI stack from the transistor up.
